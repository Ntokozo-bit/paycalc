How to use

  1. Set your Settings (gear icon): hourly rate, when overtime starts, Sunday/holiday multipliers, default break, and your week template. Save.
  2. Add Today: the blue button uses today’s date automatically. It fills start/end from your week template and default break. If you want, adjust hours/break before tapping.
  3. Edit a day: tap Edit on a card. A full-screen editor opens. You can change time, break, holiday, turn overtime on or off for that day, and even override rates just for that day.
  4. Totals: the bar under the header shows this month and the 21→20 cycle totals. Both update live as you add/edit.
  5. Download CSV: the round button at the bottom saves your data to a CSV file you can open in Excel/Sheets.
  6. Auto-Fill Cycle: in Settings, press “Auto-Fill Current Cycle” to pre-create rows for each day based on your week template (easy mass editing).

All data stays on your phone (local storage). Sundays and holidays double all worked hours, and overtime is only used on normal days.
