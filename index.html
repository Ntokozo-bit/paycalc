How to use

  1. Set your Settings: hourly rate, overtime, Sunday and holiday multipliers, pay-cycle start day, and your weekly template.
  2. Use the calendar: green means worked, dark means not worked, blue marks Sundays, and yellow marks holidays. Tap any date to add or edit details.
  3. Check the selected day: after tapping a date, the panel below the calendar shows hours, pay, and the exact rule used.
  4. Review current days: every saved day in the active cycle appears below with time, break, hours, pay, and special-day labels.
  5. History: when a pay cycle ends, old days move into History automatically and display as archived calendars.
  6. Download CSV: the round download button exports current and historical entries for Excel or Sheets.

Data is stored in this browser only. Public holidays are detected locally for South Africa, and you can still mark any date as a holiday yourself.
